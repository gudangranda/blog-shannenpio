Oda and Hototogi · MANGA.CLUB

In every class, it’s not usual for there to be one or two people who seem a little strange. In Oda’s class, there is Hototogi. Hototogi doesn’t say much and is always alone. Almost no one seems to know anything about her, which has lead to many strange rumors about her start circulating. It’s not Oda’s responsibility to befriend her, but before he realizes it, he finds himself intrigued by the mysterious Hototogi!

  • Author: Emi Fukasaku
  • Genre: One-Shot
  • Publisher: MAVO
